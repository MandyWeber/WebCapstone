What We Do

Thank you for your interest in Fine Line Entertainment. We provide an evening of entertainment and memories that you will not forget. We are available by phone and always in person to answer any questions you may have. Personal meetings are scheduled at your convenience.

All shows come with a quality sound system, a large light show to enhance the mood of the music, over 10,000 song library, and entertaining DJs to MC and keep your party happy all night long. We have a very wide range of music from the 40’s to today’s hits, consisting of country, hip hop, rock, polkas and waltz’s, 60’s, 70’s, 80’s, 90’s, 2k’s and everything in between.

For your wedding you will receive 4 hours of dance music and approximately 1 to 2 hours of dinner music. A large light show with up-lighting that can be placed throughout the venue, or you may use it for the head table. We also have a cordless microphone for use during speeches. Overtime starts after the 4-hour dance and is $150.00/hour. This is decided by you near the end of the event. There is no need to pre-pay. Ceremony music can be provided if needed for an additional fee of $195.00. For more pricing details, please contact us. A $195.00 deposit is required to secure your date. This will be deducted from your total.
